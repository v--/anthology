\subsection{Rational numbers}\label{subsec:rational_numbers}

\begin{definition}\label{def:rational_numbers}
  The \Def{rational numbers} \( \Q \) are the field of fractions\Tinyref{def:field_of_fractions} of the integers\Tinyref{def:integers}. Both operations from \( \Z \) are inherited in \( \Q \) and all nonzero elements in \( \Q \) are now invertible, which makes \( \Q \) a field.
\end{definition}
